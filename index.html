About Us

We believe divorce doesn't have to mean devastation

Divorce is one of life's most challenging transitions — emotionally, practically, and financially. Most people don't need a courtroom. They need someone in their corner who can help them understand what's happening, what needs to be done, and how to move forward without spending thousands on solicitors.

That's exactly what we do. Divorce Clinic was founded to give people practical, accessible guidance through every stage of separation — from the very first conversation right through to final order.

We don't give legal advice. We give you something just as valuable: the knowledge, tools, and support to navigate the process yourself — clearly and confidently.
